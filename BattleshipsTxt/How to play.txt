Choosing ship locations:

Select from the 4 displayed ships, from 0-3.
Input the x and y coordinates for the position of the front of the ship.
The range of x is 1 - 10. The range of y is A - J.
Input the x and y coordinates for the position of the back of the ship - make sure that it is the correct distance from the first coordinate to match the ship's size, and it is in a straight line with the first coordinate.
The ship will then be displayed on the grid, each position of the ship is marked with 'o'.

Repeat for player 2.



Choosing missile locations:

Input the x and y coordinates of where you would like to send a missile.

Repeat for player 2.

If the missile hits the other player's ship, it will be marked with an 'x' on your missile screen.
If the missile misses the other player's ship, it will be marked with '/' on your missile screen.
You can view your current ships on the lower ship screen.
If your ship has been hit, that position will be marked with an 'x'.

If all of the points of your ship have been hit, that ship is destroyed.
The game ends when all of one player's ships have been destroyed, and the other player wins.
